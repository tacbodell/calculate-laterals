This program is free and open source. You may fork and modify it to your heart's content.

This program calculates the station, offset, and finished grade elevation of all laterals at a given offset from a road centerline. It then formats it neatly and provides the results in the form of a .txt file. This has been used extensively by AlphaTec Surveyors, LTD. to automate their lateral stakeout workflow and boost profits across the company.
